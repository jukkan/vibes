Why This Matters
When hate speech increases 50% while enforcement drops 99%, and the platform deploys an AI system that generates racist imagery and defends Hitler, the message to minorities is clear: you're not safe here.

The shift to Grok-powered algorithmic recommendations means hateful content now gets better distribution, not worse. This isn't just about online harassment — it's about a systematic transformation of a major social platform into an amplification system for hate.

Over 30 civil rights groups have called on the federal government to block Grok use in public systems. 33 million users have already left. The data shows why.
